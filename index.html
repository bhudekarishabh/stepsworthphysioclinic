About Me

Dr. Gargi Shirke

Dr Gargi M. Shirke received her bachelor’s degree in Physiotherapy (B.P.Th) from the Maharashtra University of health Sciences (MUHS) in 2017. She completed her internship in 2018, during which she worked at the BARC Hospital, Mumbai and Saifee Hospital, Mumbai. During UG days, clinical postings at Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Hospital (RGMC) & Thane Civil Hospital provided vast insight into cases of multiple faculties boosting learning experience.

She had conducted a study “Comparison of risk of falls in geriatric population with and without hearing aids” which was accepted in the year 2018 and is published in the International Journal Of Current Advanced Research (IJCAR).

To serve her patients better, she has acquired certifications in Kinesiology taping, Pre and post-natal fitness, Aerobic fitness, Dry needling and more. She was also associated with the MSSI. She is also a consulting Physiotherapist at Lakecity Hospital, Thane.

Stepsworth Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation Clinic, founded by Dr Gargi Shirke with the aim of providing high standard of care and treatment, where our patient’s health and wellbeing is our top priority.
